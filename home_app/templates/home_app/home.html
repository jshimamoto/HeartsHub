Hearts Hub

About

This is a WebApp intended to make the tracking of points for Hearts more streamlined and convenient. See below for future features to be added.


Future Additions

  • Moonshot button
  • Duplicate group button (replicates players and starts new group with them)
  • Person statistics tracking (will track the player's alltime stats i.e. moonshots, points per game, average placing, etc.)
  • Group statistics: will show stats for players specific to games played within the group (average placings, most placed, etc.)
  • Animations for significant situations (moonshots, 0 points in game, etc.)
